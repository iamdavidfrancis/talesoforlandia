Time Immemorial

Curse of the White Witch

An evil force is creeping through the once prosperous land of Orlandia. Can the Bear and Raven clans defeat this mysterious foe?

Thousands of years later

Swords of Orlandia

Two princes pitted against each other in a battle of good versus evil. Who will win, the wise Horus, or his brother Hesperus?

Swords of Orlandia II

King Horus has died, and his son Validus must take his place and restore Orlandia to its former glory. Can he defeat the mysterious Emperor or will he fall victim to the Emperor's schemes?

Swords of Orlandia III

“Defeat me if you think you can, or serve me if you wish. Until that day you are all… banished from Orlandia!”

Those were the final words uttered from the treacherous mouth of Corvdius, the once supposed loyal sage to Prince Validus, only to be revealed the banished Emperor of the Yaoguai! Much time has passed and there has been much unrest in Orlandia. Many citizens of Orlandia, along with the mysterious Fae folk, have begun to form a resistance to restore freedom for all in their once glorious kingdom. The Emperor has responded by offering riches and high rank to those who would help crush the rebellion. The great battle between the Shadowfowl and the Anarchs is about to begin. Who will prevail?

Millions of years later

Crisis on Infinite Orlandias

Portals throughout history are opening and people from out of time are coming through. Team Earthquake and Team Lightning must figure out how to stop the evil Porhyrion from accomplishing their dastardly goals.
